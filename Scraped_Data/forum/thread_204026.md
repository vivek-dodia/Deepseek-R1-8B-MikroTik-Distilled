Possibly the keepalive timeout on the server is too long.From help (https://help.mikrotik.com/docs/display/ROS/OpenVPN) its definition looks kind of strange, it appears to do nothing until the timeout expires then sends ping packets one per second until either another timeout occurs or it receives a ping packet from client.I am not sure this makes sense, but anyway.Its default is 60S, perhaps reduce it to less than 45S (30S might be good) or make ping-restart on client bigger.
